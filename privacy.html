Privacy Policy for RH Toolkit

Effective Date: March 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the RH Toolkit Chrome Extension ("we", "us", or "the extension") collects, processes, stores, and shares your data. By installing and using this extension, you agree to this policy.

1. Data Collection

RH Toolkit is designed to provide Quality of Life improvements for RaterHub evaluators. To function correctly, the extension collects:

  • User Settings and Preferences: Such as your defined alert settings, timer preferences, and integration webhooks (e.g., Telegram or Discord URLs).
  • Browsing Context on raterhub.com: The extension reads elements and page states strictly on https://*.raterhub.com/evaluation/* (and related RaterHub domains) to detect task availability, timers, and interact with the required UI elements as per your configuration.
We do not collect personal identification information (names, emails, physical addresses), passwords, or browsing history outside of the specified RaterHub domain.

2. Data Processing

The data collected from the RaterHub page is processed locally on your device (within your browser). The extension processes the page's HTML elements locally to:

  • Auto-acquire tasks based on your settings.
  • Inject UI elements like timers and floating boxes.
  • Play local notification sounds when tasks are available or completed.
  • Trigger external webhook notifications if configured by the user.
No background remote processing servers or AI services are used to analyze your evaluation data.

3. Data Storage

All collected data (your settings, preferences, and webhook links) is stored locally on your device using the Chrome browser's local storage API (chrome.storage.local).

  • We do not use remote servers or cloud databases to store your personal user preferences or settings.
  • If you uninstall the extension or clear your browser data, this stored data will be permanently deleted from your device.

4. Data Sharing

We respect your privacy. RH Toolkit does not sell, trade, rent, or share your data with any third parties for commercial or advertising purposes.

Data sharing only occurs strictly under the following user-configured circumstances:

  • Webhooks to Third-Party Services (Telegram / Discord): If you manually configure the extension to send notifications via Telegram or Discord, the extension will send a payload (containing task notification context) directly to the API endpoints you provided (https://api.telegram.org/* or https://discord.com/api/webhooks/*).
No other external network requests are made with your data.
